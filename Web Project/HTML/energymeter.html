Energy Meters

Our advanced Energy Meters provide accurate monitoring of electricity consumption for homes, industries, and commercial facilities. Designed for efficiency and reliability, these meters help reduce energy wastage, optimize power usage, and lower electricity bills. Equipped with real-time data tracking, multi-circuit monitoring, and smart connectivity options, our energy meters ensure seamless integration into smart energy management systems. Perfect for industrial automation, sustainable energy solutions, and IoT-enabled monitoring, these devices deliver precise insights for better decision-making and energy savings.

Energy Meter Working Principle

Smart electric energy meters by some energy meter supplier use shunts or current transformers to turn current signals into small signals that can be used for electronic measurement, and use different types of energy meters voltage dividers or voltage transformers to turn voltage signals into small signals that can be used for electronic measurement, and then use dedicated energy measurement. The chip converts analogue signals from voltage transformers and current transformers into digital signals, and performs digital integration operations on them, and then outputs a pulse signal whose frequency is proportional to the electrical energy. The pulse signal is sent to the microcomputer for processing and then displayed on the liquid crystal. This is the general power meter working principle and Acrel energy meter factory can provide you high-quality energy meters.

Energy Meter Function

Display power, demand, voltage, current, power, time, remaining amount and other values, the number of display digits is not less than 8 digits, and the displayed decimal place can be set; the displayed value unit should adopt the national legal unit of measurement, such as kW, kvar, kWh, kvarh, V, A, etc.

Measure operating parameters such as total and split-phase active power, reactive power, power factor, split-phase voltage, split-phase current, frequency and so on.

Alarm functions include loss of voltage, loss of current, reverse sequence, overload, battery undervoltage, etc.

It has dual protection measures of programming switch and programming password to prevent unauthorized persons from performing programming operations.

Applications of Energy Meters

Energy meters play a vital role in modern power management systems by accurately measuring electricity consumption in residential, commercial, and industrial applications. From smart homes and office buildings to manufacturing plants and renewable energy installations, energy meters help monitor real-time power usage, reduce energy costs, and improve efficiency. Advanced digital and smart energy meters support load analysis, peak demand monitoring, billing accuracy, and integration with solar power systems and smart grids. By enabling data-driven energy management, energy meters enhance operational performance, ensure regulatory compliance, and promote sustainable energy solutions for businesses and households alike.
